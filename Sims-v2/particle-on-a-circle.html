About this simulation

A quantum particle confined to a ring of radius \(R=1\), in natural units \(\hbar = 1\), \(2m = 1\). You can explore different angular-momentum eigenstates \(n\), their energies, and the time evolution of the wavefunction on the circle.

Key relations:
\[E_n = \frac{n^2\hbar^2}{2mR^2}, \quad \psi_n(\phi) = \frac{1}{\sqrt{2\pi}} e^{in\phi}, \quad L_z = n\hbar\]

States with \(n\) and \(-n\) are energetically degenerate but carry opposite angular momentum.
